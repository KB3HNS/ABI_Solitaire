Klondike 2 - Game Rules
General:
    Klondike is played using 52 cards - Ace through King in four suits.  The Ace is the low card and the king is the high card. 

The Board:
    The board is divided up into 2 sections:
       1:  The main board - 7 columbs of cards dealt as follows:
             1 - Columb 1:  2 down, 3 up
             2 - Columb 2:  4 down, 5 up
             3 - Columb 3:  6 down, 6 up
             4 - Columb 4:  5 down, 6 up
             5 - Columb 5:  3 down, 5 up
             6 - Columb 6:  1 down, 4 up
             7 - Columb 7:  2 up

       2:  The Goal Section:  4 columbs to move the cards to.

The Main Board:
    Cards are dealt to the main board one row at a time.  The first row is dealt from left to right, then the secont row is dealt from right to left.  The down cards and the first up card in each row are dealt before any other up cards are dealt.  This will quickly yield an alternating pattern when dealt manually.  Cards are moved from columb to columg by selecting one card in a columb and then placing it on the bottom of another columb.  Cards operate in alternating colors in descending order.  Ex:  a black 3 can go on a red 4 reguardless of what is on top of the 3.  When this happens, the whole columb, from the 3 on down, is appended to the columb that terminated with the 4.  When there are no more face up cards in a columb, the first face down card may be turned face up.  This is done by clicking on the card or using the arrow kays to turn it face up.  When there are no more face down cards left the columb is empty.  Only a King can be placed on an empty columb.  Like other moves, the king does not have to be the last card in a stack in order to move it to an empty columb.  When a king is moved, everything that is on top of the King follows it. 

The Goal Section:
    The objective of Klondike 2 is to move all of the cards from main board to the goal section.  There are 4 positions in the place board, one for each suit.  Cards are moved up to the goal section in ascending order starting with the Ace.  Once a card has been moved to the place board, it is considered out of play and can not be moved back to the main board.  When all 52 cards have been moved to the goal section, the game is won.
