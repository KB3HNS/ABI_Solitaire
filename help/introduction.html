Introduction:

    There are many solitaire games out there.  This is one of them.   This game, in particular started out as many other good computer based projects:  in a basement (or at least a back room in a one story house).  Solitaire was origionally written as a QuickBasic program.  Not much happened until recently when I decided to port it form something archaic to something at least hinting of modern.  Now, this game is written almost entirely in C++.  The individual games are platform independant classes that rely on a series of routines that must be rewritten to become platform specific.  For more information about this, please see the developer notes.

    Currently, this build of Solitaire supports only these 3 games:
       Streets and Alleys
       War!
       Klondike 2
       Pyrimid
       Calculation
    More to come!
