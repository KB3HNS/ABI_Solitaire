Streets and Alleys - Game Rules
General:
    Streets and alleys is played using 52 cards - 2 - Ace in four suits.  The Ace counts as both a low and high card and cards cycle through it.  ex:  K, A, 2. 

The Board:
    The board is divided up into 3 sections:
       1:  The main board - 3 rows of 5 columns, up to 3 cards deep.
       2:  The "Free" board - 1 row of 3 columns, up to 3 cards deep.
       3:  The "Place" board - 2 rows of 2 columns.

The Main Board:
    In the main board, cards can be placed in descending order alternating colors.  ex:  Queen of hearts, Jack of clubs, 10 of diamonds.  Each pile can have up to 3 cards in it.  Once the last card in a pile has been removed, that pile may no longer be used.  It starts out completely full.

The "Free" Board:
    Cards in the free board can be placed in ascending or descending order alternating colors.  ex:   5 spades, 6 hearts, 7 spades.  Having both ascending and descending in a pile is acceptable.  ex:  3 diamonds, 4 clubs, 3 hearts.  Once a pile in the free board has been depleted, any card can be placed to restart a pile.  At the beginning of the game, each pile in the free board contains only 2 of the 3 possible cards in it. 

The "Place" board:
    The objective of Streets and Alleys is to move all of the cards from the place and main boards to the free board.  There are 4 positions in the place board, one for each suit.  The first position is automatically dealt a card to begin the game.  To start the other 3 positions, you must place the same value card, of a different suit.  ex:  if the first position began with a 8 of clubs, the 2nd position must be started with any other 8.  Once a card has been moved to the place board, it is considered out of play and can not be moved back to the main or free boards.  Cards are moved to the place board in ascending order by suit.  ex:  7 diamond, 8 diamond, 9 diamond.  Each time a card is moved to the place board, the score increases by one.  Each game starts out with a score of 1.  When the score reaches 52, the game has been won. 

Good Luck!  And, Enjoy! :-)
