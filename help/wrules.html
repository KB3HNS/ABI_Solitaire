War - Game Rules:
General:
    War is played with 52 cards:  2 through Ace in each f the 4 suits.  Each player reveals the top card of their deck.  The player whose card is the greatest value wins both cards.  Cards range from 2 to Ace, with the Ace being the top card.  If the card values are equal, the players enter a "War."  If a player has exhausted their cards, they flip their discard pile over and that becomes their play deck.  The game is over when one player has all 52 cards.  That player is the winner.

War:
    In a war, each player takes 2 cards from the top their deck and places them face down on top of the cards that are at war.  Then they take the next card and place it face up.  If a player does not have enough cards, the last card is placed face up.  The player whose card is the greatest value wins the war, and all the cards involved.  In the event that both players have cards of the same value, the War repeats until one player wins. 

War in Solitaire:
    Cards are selected by clicking on them or pressing the enter key.  You may view any of your cards, including those in your play stack, but you can not view cards in your opponent's play stack.  Selecting a card will move it to the center of the board.  The PC will then reveal it's next card.  The cards are displayed for 1 second and then the winner is calculated.  In the event of a war, the program will draw your cards for you and display them for 1 second before proceding. 
Note*  There is NO undo in War! (Restarts are acceptable).
