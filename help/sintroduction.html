Streets and Alleys - Introduction:
    Streets and Alleys was developed some time in the early 80's by my Grandmother.. I believe.  Either way, I was not there so I really can't tell you.  I origionally wrote this game in QBasic using text based sprites for the cards and a small assembly program for the mouse.  Recently, I finally decided to port it to something more modern, C++.  This is one of the first major projects that I have tried to write in a while. 

    In the future, I plan to move much of the drawing functions to a library, and possibly make libs out of the game classes.  Also, I will most likely write more games, perhaps a 4 player version of war and a spades game.
