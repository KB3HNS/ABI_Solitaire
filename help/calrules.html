Calculation - Game Rules
General:
    Calculation is played using 52 cards - Ace through King in four suits.  The King is a high card, and the Ace is low - kind of...

The Board:
    The board is divided up into 3 sections:
       1:  The main board - Cards can be placed in any one of four columbs in any order.

       2:  The goal section - When all cards have been moved up here, the game is won.

       3:  The draw pile.  The draw pile initially contains the remaining 48 cards.

The Main Board:
       The main board consists of 4 empty columbs.  Cards can be moved from the draw pile to any one of the four columbs in any order.  Cards must be stored here until ready to be moved to the top.  Although there are no criteria to placing cards here, only the last card in a columb may be played.  Therefore care should be taken when placing cards here as to not block cards necessary for play by ones that cannot be played soon.

The Goal Section:
       The goal section begins with four columbs containing the Ace of Spades, 2 of Diamonds, 3 of Clubs, and 4 of Hearts respectively.  Thus, the score begins with 4.  The objective of the game is to move all of the cards here, in order.  In order to move a card here it's value must be the next in sequence.  The sequence starts with Ace then 2 - 10 then Jack, Queen, and King.  Then the pattern repeats.  The suit of the card does not really matter, what matters is the series of the card.  The first columb, beginning with the Ace increments by 1.  To move cards here you must place any 2, then any 3, and so forth.  In the second columb, the sequence increments by 2.  Thus you must move a 4, then a 6, etc.  The third columb increases to 3 and the fourth, 4.  Each pile is completed when a King has been placed on it.  The game has been won when all four piles have kings on them. 

The Draw Pile:
       Cards are turned over one at a time from the draw pile.  The cards must either be moved to the goal section, if possible, or to the main board.  The draw pile starts with the remaining 48 cards after the cards for the goal have been removed. 

The Goal:
       The goal of calculation is to move all of the cards to the top of the board, the goal section in order. 

Good Luck!  And, Enjoy! :-)
