Klondike 2 - Introduction:
    Klondike 2 to the best of my knowledge was developed some time in the early 80's by my father as a modification to the classic klondike game (what most people call solitaire).  The biggest advantage of this game over it's pretecessor is that it allows the player to make many more choices as to how to play the cards.  This makes the game much more difficult.  Thankfully, this game supports unlimited undo which any avid player of this game will need desperately.  I have found this to be the case.  Well, I hope that you enjoy playing the game and of course, good luck.
