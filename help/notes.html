Game Independent Notes:

    These notes do not count as fact and may be changed by each individual game. 
The Cards:
    The game contains 56 card images:
        2 - Ace in 4 suits (52)
        2 Jokers
       An "E" card: 
       The back of the cards: 
    The "E" card is actually not a card.  The game uses this image to denote where a card can go but where no card currently exists.  The "E" effectively stands for empty.  A card whose white and black colors have been reversed means that the card has been selected.    In order to move a card, you must select it, and then move to where the card should be placed either using the mouse or the arrow keys and press the left mouse button or the enter key.  Some games will interpret a card being clicked on as both a select and a play.  In this case, the program will automatically move the card to where it needs to go.

The Games:
    In general, the objective of any game is to move all of the cards to one place.  Usually, cards will have to remain suited or alternate black and red and procede in an order of value.  (Ex:  4 hearts 3 clubs 2 diamonds).  The Ace may count as a low card, high card, or both.  (ex:  Ace, 2, 3; Queen, King Ace; King, Ace, 2)
